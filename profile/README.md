## Overview

DaVinci Resolve Studio Advanced is Blackmagic Design's all-in-one professional video production platform that combines editing, color grading, visual effects, and audio post-production in a single application used across Hollywood film production, broadcast television, and commercial post-production.

The color grading suite is the most sophisticated available in any editing application, with primary and secondary correction tools, node-based processing, HDR grading capabilities, and film emulation that give colorists the depth of control that professional color work demands.

Fusion visual effects integration provides a complete node-based compositing environment within the same application as the edit, enabling complex VFX, motion graphics, and visual effects work without round-tripping to separate compositing software.

Fairlight audio post-production delivers a complete digital audio workstation within the Resolve environment, handling dialogue cleanup, music mixing, sound design, and final mix delivery for any production format.

For colorists, editors, and post-production professionals who want a single platform that handles every stage of post-production at the highest professional standard, DaVinci Resolve Studio Advanced is the production environment that Hollywood uses and that independent creators can access at an exceptional price point.

## Performance

DaVinci Resolve Studio balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
